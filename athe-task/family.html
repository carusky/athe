Kids Dentistry:

In order to help them become accustomed to sitting in our chair and having someone look into their mouth, we welcome youngsters as young as one year old to our clinic. In addition, we provide warm, inviting environments where parents, young children, and older ones can learn the advantages of good oral hygiene practices like brushing and flossing twice a day and the significance of routine checkups. Establishing these routines at an early age significantly lessens the anxiety related to dental visits.

Because it is difficult to reach the dots and lines (pits and grooves) on your teeth with a toothbrush, cavities tend to form there more quickly. This is especially true for young children who are just learning how to brush. For this reason, we advise applying fluoride and fissure sealants in addition to practicing good oral hygiene.

The process of fissure sealing involves applying a plastic coating to the tooth's pits and grooves to smooth out the surface and make it easier to brush. This keeps food from being caught in the tooth. Since it only takes a few minutes, kids can endure it well and there's no need for drilling. Conversely, fluoride application involves covering teeth with a large concentration of fluoride. It has been demonstrated that both of these therapies can slow the onset of cavities in your child's teeth.
