Happy, Healthy Smiles

Visiting the dentist should be an enjoyable experience, and we plan to make every visit a relaxing and memorable one for you! We combine skill, modern technology, and excellent customer service here at Right Dental Care to provide solutions that leave our clients feeling confident about the health and appearance of their smiles. Our facility provides international standards of care under strict hygienic conditions with very talented, professional dentists and staff who work together as a team to give you the right care you deserve. ...

We know when our patients are well informed, they can prevent oral health problems so we spend time to educate you on how to prevent these and ensure you fully understand any diagnosis and available treatment options before helping you decide what best suits your needs. Dental treatment is always most affordable when attended to early, so please do not sit on any problem you may know about and have us check to see if there are any you may be unaware of. We offer flexible payment packages to help you receive care without worrying about the effect on your pocket. Book an appointment today for a consultation and you will be glad you did!

Meet the Dentist

Dr. Asare Zuckerberg

Dr. Afful has always felt a deep calling to improve confidence through smiles.

She attended the University of Ghana where she received her Bachelor of Medical Sciences and Bachelor of Dental Surgery degrees.

After completing her internships at the Korle-Bu Teaching Hospital and Greater Accra Regional Hospital (Ridge Hospital), Dr Afful joined the private sector focusing on general and cosmetic dentistry.

She regularly attends courses both internationally and locally to bring best practices in dentistry, and a high quality of care to her clients. When she is not seeing to clients, she uses various platforms to raise awareness on good oral health care practices.

She is an avid reader, enjoys spending time with family, photography and playing tennis
